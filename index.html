About Us

- Harshada Sunil Gurav

With over 10 years of professional experience, the Founder & Principal Designer of Espacio Designs has built a reputation for creating refined, contemporary interiors rooted in clarity, function, and precision. Trained through hands‑on practice across luxury residential and commercial projects, their work reflects a deep understanding of spatial planning, materials, and detailing.

Driven by a belief that great design must be both visually disciplined and intuitively livable, the founder approaches each project with a balance of creative intent and technical rigor. From concept development to on‑site execution, every space is carefully crafted to achieve screen‑perfect outcomes where proportion, materiality, and light work in quiet harmony.
